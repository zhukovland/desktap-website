Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Overview

Desktap is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app operates entirely over your local Wi-Fi network, and we do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers.

Data Collection

Desktap does not collect personal information. No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry. The app works offline within your local network.

Local Communication

Desktap communicates between your iPhone/iPad and your Mac exclusively over your local Wi-Fi network. Commands, button configurations, and all other data travel directly between your devices. Nothing passes through our servers.

iCloud Sync

If you use iCloud sync, your button configurations are stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. This data is managed entirely by Apple under their privacy policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

In-App Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, see, or store any payment information, billing details, or financial data.

MCP Integration

When you use AI-powered button creation via MCP (Model Context Protocol), communication happens directly between your AI assistant (e.g., Claude Desktop) and the Desktap Agent on your Mac. No data is routed through Desktap servers.

Third-Party Services

Desktap does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Desktap does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal data, no special provisions are needed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@desktap.app.
